About Me

Nestled in the heart of Edinburg, Loud and Clear Records is more than just a record store; it's a vibrant community for music lovers of all genres. Established in 2024, we are passonate about curating a diverse selection of vinyl records, cassettes, and CDs, catering to both seasoned collectors and newcomers ear to explore the world of music.

Our Story Founded by L.C.R, a lifelong music enthusiast and collector, Loud and Clear Records started as a small dream fueled by a love for vinyl. Over the years, our shop has transformed into a beloved destination for music aficionados seeking rare finds, classic albums, and the latest releases. Our goal is to create a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home, whether you’re digging for a vintage gem or simply browsing through our latest arrivals.
